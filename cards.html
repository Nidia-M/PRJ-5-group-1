KadeRock

Kaderock, the pop festival around the famous Musicon Music Centre in The Hague, has been around since 1996. The careers of almost all well-known pop and urban artists from The Hague in recent decades started there. This annual gathering of pop generations is part of the intangible heritage of the Netherlands and offers a unique opportunity to get acquainted with the past, present and future of The Hague.
